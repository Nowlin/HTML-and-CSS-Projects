Your Approach!

Should feel natural, it should end with your dominant hand and non dominant leg just short of the foul line.

Your aim!

The common area to aim at is the 2nd arrow from your dominant side. You should avoid looking at it until just before you start your approach, then lock onto it until after you release the ball
